About Us

Roompal, a proptech startup, is an innovative online booking platform specifically designed for the room rental industry. It shall be implemented and operate in close coordination with local government units (LGUs), higher education institutions (HEIs), room providers, and students. The primary goal of Roompal is to streamline property management and marketing for room providers while providing direct market access to renters.

We help YOU acquire safe, affordable, convenient housing by connecting YOU to your preferred accommodation.
